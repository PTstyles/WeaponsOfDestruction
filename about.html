Welcome to Weapons of Destruction

Weapons of Destruction is an Alliance guild on Gnomeregan-US. We pride ourselves on a large community atmosphere with players ranging from serious raiders/pvpers to casual/social members. WoD is lead by GM Magepowerftw (or Andy) he took over the guild in early 2016 and has grown it from 1 raid team and under 30 members to 4 thriving raid teams and over 300 active members. Backing Andy are Addictionz, Struggles, and Swarfie acting as officers and leaders in the Guild. Between the 4 of them they bring over 30 years of WoW experience in all aspects of the game. WoD is currently progressing through Emerald Nightmare with 3 groups 7/7 Normal and 2 groups 1/7 Heroic after the first week of raiding! Weapons is always looking to grow its community and find players that will add to the success of any and all raid teams. For more information please contact one of the officers found below!
